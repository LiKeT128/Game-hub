UI art direction rules:

- Build a premium dark interface with dossier / classified archive / operations center aesthetics.
- Use elegant typography, restrained color, and strong card hierarchy.
- Avoid generic purple gradients, glowing AI blobs, childish cyberpunk, fantasy motifs, and startup dashboard clichés.
- Prioritize readability, tension, and polish.
- Use subtle motion and dramatic reveal transitions.
- Use UI-first immersion rather than heavy illustration dependence.
- If images are used, they must be sparse, atmospheric, and intentional.
