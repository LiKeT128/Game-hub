After generating the solution, perform a self-review with these steps:

1. List the 10 most likely weaknesses in your implementation.
2. Identify any areas where the multiplayer sync could desync.
3. Identify any parts of the UX that may confuse first-time players.
4. Identify any overly complex mechanics that should be simplified for MVP.
5. Identify any missing deployment or environment details.
6. Revise the solution to fix the most important issues before finalizing.

Do not skip this review.
